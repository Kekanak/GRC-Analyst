# what is the name of the career path? 
GRC Analyst. 
# What are the main responsibility of someone in this role? 
The main responsibility of GRC is to enable the business to take calculated risks, not to say "no" to everything.
# What skills are required for this career? 
Communication, basic tech literacy, risk math
# Which tools or technologies are commonly used? 
Ai assistance, Thehive
# Why did you choose this path? 
Compliance is legally mandated. Even in a recession, companies must pass audits, so the job is highly secure.
# What steps will you take to become successful in this field? 
Learn the frameworks, get the right entry ticket.
